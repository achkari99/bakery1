OUR STORY

Born from awareness, dedicated to well-being

Golden Sweet was founded in 2019, during the COVID-19 pandemic, when health and immunity became global priorities. This moment shaped our vision: indulgence should never come at the expense of well-being. From day one, we chose to create 100% natural, sugar-free pastries designed to be safe, balanced, and bring true peace of mind.

2019

The first sugar-free, healthy recipes

Complete removal of refined sugar and harmful ingredients, replaced with natural oils such as argan, olive, and coconut, and flours including barley, almond, and corn.

2021

Quality and consistency

Continuous refinement of taste and texture, alongside strict standards for ingredient sourcing, preparation, and food safety.

2023

Controlled growth

Expansion of production and menu offerings while preserving freshness, safety, and our sugar-free, natural philosophy.

2026

A global vision

Expansion through franchising, bringing the Golden Sweet experience to new cities while maintaining the same quality, recipes, and standards worldwide.
