Our Process

Every roll goes through a careful artisan process

1

Mixing

Premium plant-based ingredients are mixed by hand following our signature recipes developed over years of experimentation.

2

Slow Rise

Our dough rests for 12-24 hours for optimal flavor development and that signature fluffy texture.

3

Shaping

Each roll is hand-shaped with care, ensuring the perfect spiral and generous filling distribution.

4

Baking

Fresh from the oven multiple times daily. We bake in small batches for maximum freshness.

5

Glazing

Warm rolls receive their signature glaze - from classic vanilla to pistachio cream.

6

Delivery

Carefully packaged and delivered warm to your door, any time of day or night.

Quality Ingredients

We source the best plant-based ingredients from trusted suppliers

Organic Flours

Premium wheat flour from certified organic Moroccan mills for the perfect dough texture.

Ceylon Cinnamon

True Ceylon cinnamon imported for its delicate, sweet flavor - never harsh cassia.

Plant Butters

High-quality vegan butter that melts perfectly for flaky, tender pastries.

Local Nuts

Pistachios from Azemmour, almonds from the Atlas - supporting Moroccan farmers.

Hygiene & Safety

Temperature Control

Trained Staff

All team members are trained in food safety and hygiene practices. Regular refresher courses.
