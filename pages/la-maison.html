The Cinnamona Story

Our Journey

A Story of Passion & Plant-Based Innovation

It all started in 2019, when Mona began baking cinnamon rolls in her small kitchen in Marrakech. What began as treats for friends and family quickly became a local sensation.

Inspired by her love for plant-based living and traditional pastry techniques, Mona developed recipes that proved vegan desserts could be just as indulgent as their traditional counterparts.

Today, Cinnamona by Mona operates 24/7, serving freshly baked rolls, cannelés, and pastéis de nata to locals, hotels, and visitors from around the world.

2019

First Home Rolls

One box, six soft rolls and word-of-mouth that spread across Marrakech in weeks.

2021

Assif C Workshop

Installation of our plant-based lab and launch of pistachio, amlou and Moroccan coffee flavors.

2022

Night Team Established

24/7 production to satisfy hotels, cafés and late-night neighbors across the city.

2024

Expanded Menu

Arrival of cannelés, pastéis de nata and colorful lattes to complement our iconic rolls.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

100% Plant-Based

Every ingredient we use is vegan. No exceptions, no compromises. We prove that plant-based can be indulgent.

Freshly Baked Daily

Our pastries are made multiple times a day, ensuring you always get that just-out-of-the-oven experience.

Local & Seasonal

We source from Moroccan producers: pistachios from Azemmour, amlou from Souss, coffee from Guéliz roasters.

Community First

Every roll funds local initiatives. We train young pastry chefs and support Marrakech artisans.
