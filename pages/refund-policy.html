Customer Rights

Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Our Commitment to Quality

At Golden Sweet, we are committed to providing fresh, high-quality pastries made with 100% natural ingredients. Your satisfaction is our priority. This Refund & Return Policy complies with Moroccan consumer protection law (Law 31-08).

2. Perishable Food Products

Due to the fresh and perishable nature of our products, we have specific policies regarding refunds and returns:

  • All pastries are freshly baked and prepared to order
  • Products contain no preservatives and have a limited shelf life
  • For food safety and hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns of consumed products

3. Eligible Refund Situations

We will provide a refund or replacement in the following cases:

3.1 Product Quality Issues

  • Products received damaged or spoiled
  • Products that do not meet our quality standards
  • Products with visible defects or contamination
  • Incorrect products delivered (different from what was ordered)

3.2 Delivery Issues

  • Order not delivered within the promised timeframe
  • Missing items from your order
  • Order delivered to wrong address due to our error

3.3 Allergies or Dietary Requirements

  • Product contains undisclosed allergens
  • Product does not meet dietary specifications confirmed at time of order

4. Non-Refundable Situations

We cannot provide refunds in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after order completion
  • Customer unavailable at delivery address
  • Incorrect address provided by customer
  • Products properly delivered but not consumed within recommended timeframe
  • Personal taste preferences
  • Products damaged after delivery due to customer handling

5. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are entitled to a refund, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Contact Us Immediately

Report the issue within 24 hours of delivery:

  • Phone: +212 603 981 438
  • Email: contact@goldensweet.ma
  • WhatsApp: +212 637 629 395

Step 2: Provide Evidence

To process your refund request, please provide:

  • Order number
  • Clear photos of the product and issue
  • Description of the problem
  • Delivery receipt (if applicable)

Step 3: Resolution

We will review your request and respond within 48 hours with one of the following solutions:

  • Full refund to original payment method
  • Replacement order at no additional cost
  • Store credit for future orders

6. Refund Processing Time

Once approved:

  • Cash payments: Refund within 7 business days
  • Bank transfer: Refund within 7-10 business days
  • Credit/Debit card: Refund within 10-14 business days (depending on your bank)
  • Store credit: Issued immediately

7. Order Cancellation Policy

7.1 Before Preparation

You may cancel your order free of charge if:

  • The order has not yet been prepared (usually within 30 minutes of order placement)
  • Full refund will be processed

7.2 After Preparation Has Started

If your order is already being prepared:

  • Cancellation may not be possible
  • We will assess on a case-by-case basis
  • Partial refund may be offered

7.3 Custom Orders

For personalized or custom orders:

  • Cancellation must be made at least 48 hours before delivery time
  • Cancellations with less than 48 hours notice may incur a 50% cancellation fee
  • No refunds for custom orders canceled within 24 hours of delivery

8. Wholesale and B2B Orders

Different terms may apply for wholesale and business clients. Please refer to your specific contract or contact us at contact@goldensweet.ma.

9. Consumer Rights (Moroccan Law 31-08)

Under Moroccan consumer protection law, you have the right to:

  • Receive products that conform to the description
  • Request a refund or replacement for defective products
  • Be informed of product ingredients and allergens
  • Fair and transparent business practices

10. Exceptions and Special Circumstances

In cases of:

  • Force majeure: Extreme weather, natural disasters, or other uncontrollable events
  • National emergencies: Government restrictions or public health orders

We reserve the right to modify this policy temporarily. Affected customers will be notified directly.
