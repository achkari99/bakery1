Frequently Asked Questions

Allergens

Yes! All our products are 100% plant-based. We use no animal products whatsoever - no dairy, no eggs, no honey. Our vegan recipes are just as delicious and indulgent as traditional pastries.

Most of our pastries contain wheat flour and therefore gluten. We don't currently offer gluten-free options, but we're working on it! Contact us for specific allergen information.

Many of our products contain tree nuts (almonds, pistachios, pecans, hazelnuts). Our kitchen handles nuts regularly, so we cannot guarantee nut-free products. Please inform us of any allergies when ordering.
