OUR STORY

Born from awareness, dedicated to well-being

Golden Sweet was founded in 2019, during the COVID-19 pandemic, when health and immunity became global priorities. This moment shaped our vision: indulgence should never come at the expense of well-being. From day one, we chose to create 100% natural, sugar-free pastries designed to be safe, balanced, and bring true peace of mind.

2019

The first healthy recipes

From the start, every recipe was built on six pillars of healthy indulgence: lactose-free, sugar-free, no white flour used, using natural fermentation, gluten-free, and free from hydrogenated oil. We rebuilt our pastries with natural oils like argan, olive, and coconut, and alternative flours such as barley, almond, and corn.

2021

Quality and consistency

Continuous refinement of taste and texture, alongside strict standards for ingredient sourcing, preparation, and food safety.

2023

Controlled growth

Expansion of production and menu offerings while preserving freshness, safety, and our sugar-free, natural philosophy.

2026

A global vision

Expansion through franchising, bringing the Golden Sweet experience to new cities while maintaining the same quality, recipes, and standards worldwide.

02 Raw Materials

Honey, Our Natural Sweetener

We choose Moroccan honeys harvested in small batches for their purity, floral notes, and gentle sweetness.

It gives our pastries a rounded sweetness without refined sugar, while keeping flavors bright and balanced.

3
02 Raw Materials

Chocolate, Deep and Pure

We blend rich cocoa with roasted nuts to create a smooth, indulgent taste without added sugar.

The result is a velvety chocolate profile that stays light, clean, and satisfying.

02 Raw Materials

Amlou, Moroccan Soul

Our amlou is crafted from roasted nuts and seeds, gently blended into a creamy, nutrient-rich paste.

It adds a toasty, comforting richness that makes every bite feel grounded and authentic.
